Introduction

As you recall, a writer adapts the messages to the needs of the audience by organizing the information in a particular sequence and through maintaining an even, conversational tone of good will. In week two, you learned about tone. This week you will learn about patterns of organization. You will also learn about routine emails and memos.

Learning Objectives

At the end of this lesson, you will be able to:

  • distinguish between the indirect and direct patterns of organization,
  • identify the elements of an email and memo.
